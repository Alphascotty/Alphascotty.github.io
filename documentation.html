Below are the issues I encountered and how I solved them:

  • Poles Getting More Spaced Out After 10
    Issue: Obstacles seemed to be spaced further apart after a certain point in the game.
    Fix: Obstacle Spacing Adjustment: Added a fixed spacing interval (150 pixels) between sets of obstacles to maintain consistent spacing regardless of game speed.

  • Crash Sound Played After Game Over Alert
    Issue: The crash sound was played only after the game-over alert was shown, which was not the desired behavior.
    Fix: Timing Adjustment: Used setTimeout to delay the game-over alert by 500 milliseconds, allowing the crash sound to play completely before the game-over alert appears.

  • Game Speed Increase Frequency Issue
    Issue: The game speed increase was set to occur every 10000 units, which was too infrequent.
    Fix: Adjusted Speed Increase: Changed the frequency of speed increase to every 1000 units by modifying the speed increase logic to reflect every 10 score points (since 100 frames per point).

  • Background Music Pause and Resume Functionality
    Issue: Background music needed to pause and resume correctly with the pause button.
    Fix: Pause and Resume: Implemented togglePause() function to pause and resume the game and background music. The background music is paused when the game is paused and resumed when the game continues.

  • Adjusting the Character Fit with Obstacles
    Issue: The gap between poles was not large enough to ensure the character could fit through.
    Fix: Obstacle Gap Adjustment: Modified the gap between obstacles to be at least 50 + myGamePiece.height to ensure the character fits through comfortably.

  • Crash Sound Timing During Collision
    Issue: Crash sound was not playing immediately upon collision.
    Fix: Immediate Crash Sound Play: Ensured the crash sound is played immediately when a collision is detected, before stopping the game.
